Frequently Ask Questions

How can I cancel my Reservation or Order?

All Reservations and Orders can be canceled via the means they were made within 24hr

How can I change delivery address after order placement?

To change the delivery address after order placement, simply go to your app, find the order, and select "Change Address" option. Then input the new address and confirm.

How can I contact my delivery person?

Contact your delivery person through the app's messaging feature or by calling the provided phone number associated with the delivery.

My payments is declining.What should I do?

If your payment is declining, ensure you have sufficient funds, check for any errors in card details, and try again. If issues persist, contact your bank or try an alternative payment method
